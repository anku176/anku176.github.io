FAQ  

Q: What vegetables are suitable to put through Veggetti?

Veggetti works on any food that is 6.5cm or smaller in diameter. The best vegetables to use are zucchini, carrots, radishes, potatoes, beetroot and squash.

Q: How do I choose the thickness?

Easy, just insert your vegetable of choice at either end of the Veggetti - "thin" and "thick" are marked on either end.

Q: How do I clean my Veggetti?

Simply put it in your dishwasher after using.
